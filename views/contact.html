A few ways to get to jLab

From İzmir Adnan Menderes (ADB) Airport 1

Probably easiest way to reach jLab using public transportation is using the subway (IZBAN) just at the airport. You can either buy 3-5 credit ticket from the kiosk located at the gate of the station.
Note that you are to direct to Aliağa direction, and you will get off at Halkapınar station. You will change direction within the station to get to İzmir Metro subway, for Fahrettin Altay bus-stop direction.
Now you have two options:

  • Get the bus 736 from the terminal. You may have a look at the ring times of the bus line from provided link.
    Important note: If you are still in 90 minutes from first set foot to IZBAN, previous and this ride is free!
  • Get the minibus, a direct (maybe a bit slow) service to the campus. It will cost 5₺ or 1,5 € (April, 2016).

From İzmir Adnan Menderes (ADB) Airport 2

If you want the least effort way to reach jLab, we advise you to take a yellow cab.
It should cost around 130₺ or ~40€ (April, 2016) . Most of the cab drivers can communicate with you, but here is excerpt in Turkish roughly translates to: "please take me to IZTECH campus, Gülbahçe-Urla, from the highway D300, than take the autobahn O-32. Then please take Karaburun exit."

İzmir Yüksek Teknoloji Enstitüsü Gülbahçe-Urla Kampüsüne gitmek istiyorum. Lütfen D300 çevre yolundan Çeşme otobanını (O-32) kullanın. Karaburun çıkışınından kampüse ulaşabilirsiniz.
