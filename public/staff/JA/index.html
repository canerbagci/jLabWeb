Mission

Why

I believe that bioinformatics should first and foremost facilitate biological and medical research. It should enable researchers to focus on their area of expertise removing the burden of data handling and analysis from their shoulders.

How

Using a mixture of employing existing tools, scripting, developing software, and preparing data analytics workflows, we perform data analysis in a transparent and reproducible manner.

What

MicroRNA analysis, proteomics data analysis, and some part of genomics data analysis (de novo assembly, differential expression analysis, and annotation) are performed in collaboration with colleagues.
